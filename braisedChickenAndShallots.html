Ingredients

3 pounds chicken

salt and ground pepper

4 large shallots, peeled and quartered

2-3 teapspoons of olive oil

1 head of garlic, separated, peeled and crushed

2 teaspoons thyme

1 teaspoons sage

1/2 teaspoons rosemary

1 bay leaf

1&1/2 cup white wine

2 tablespoons mustard

Instructions

Season chicken with salt and pepper.

Heat 2 tablespoons oil in skillet over medium heat.

Sear chicken in oil 5-6 minutes.

Flip chicken.

Sear other side of chicken in oil 5-6 minutes.

Remove chicken from skillet.

If pan has little oil left, add tablespoon oil.

Add shallots, garlic, thume, sage, rosemary, and bayleaf to skillet.

Saute until aromatic, five minutes on low heat.

Pour wine and mustard in bowl and mix.

Return chicken to skillet.

Add mustard mixture to skillet.

Let skillet simmer gently for 15-30 minutes.
